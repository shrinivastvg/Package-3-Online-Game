Case Study Scenario: Preparation of ECSMF for a Water Supply Project in a Small Town on the Banks of River Kaveri in Tamil Nadu

Background:

A small town on the banks of the River Kaveri is experiencing increasing demand for drinking water due to population growth and industrial development. The town proposes a water supply improvement project, including the development of an intake structure at the river, a water treatment plant (WTP), and a distribution network.

Objective:

Develop and implement an Environmental, Climate Change, and Social Management Framework (ECSMF) for this project to address environmental, climate, and social risks.

Learning Progression Through Game Levels:

The game progresses through Basic, Intermediate, and Advanced levels, helping participants gradually master ECSMF preparation.

  • Basic level: Covers foundational concepts like definitions, laws, and ECSMF objectives.
  • Intermediate level: Introduces practical scenarios on risk categorization, stakeholder engagement, and grievance mechanisms.
  • Advanced level: Focuses on designing mitigation strategies and compliance monitoring.

By completing all levels, participants gain a comprehensive understanding of ECSMF and the skills to address risks in water supply projects.
